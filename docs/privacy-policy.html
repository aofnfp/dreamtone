DreamTone — Privacy Policy

Last Updated: March 29, 2026

Developer: Abraham Oladotun Foundation NFP

1. Overview

DreamTone ("the App") is a sleep sounds and relaxation application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about the data we collect.

2. Information We Collect

Data Stored Locally

  • Sound preferences and custom mixes
  • Sleep timer settings
  • App preferences and settings

This data is stored on your device and is not transmitted to our servers.

Analytics & Crash Reporting

  • Anonymous crash reports via Sentry (device model, OS version, crash stack traces)
  • No personally identifiable information is included in crash reports

Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob to display advertisements. AdMob may collect:

  • Device advertising identifier (IDFA/GAID)
  • IP address (for general location)
  • Device and app interaction data

You can opt out of personalized ads in your device settings.

In-App Purchases

Subscription management is handled by RevenueCat. RevenueCat processes:

  • Anonymous app user IDs
  • Purchase and subscription status

Payment processing is handled entirely by Apple App Store or Google Play Store.

3. How We Use Information

  • To provide and improve the App's functionality
  • To diagnose and fix crashes and bugs
  • To display advertisements (free tier)
  • To manage subscription status

4. Third-Party Services

  • Google AdMob: Google Privacy Policy
  • RevenueCat: RevenueCat Privacy Policy
  • Sentry: Sentry Privacy Policy
  • Expo/EAS: Expo Privacy Policy

5. Data Retention

Local data is retained until you delete the App or clear its data. Crash reports are retained for 90 days. AdMob and RevenueCat follow their respective data retention policies.

6. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

7. Your Rights

You can delete all local data by uninstalling the App. To request deletion of crash report data or exercise other privacy rights, contact us at the email below.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App and on this page.
