DreamTone Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 28, 2026

Overview

DreamTone ("the App") is a sleep sounds application developed by the Abraham Oladotun Foundation ("we", "us", "our"). We are committed to protecting your privacy. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

Data We Collect

DreamTone is designed to work fully offline. We do not collect, store, or transmit any personal data to our servers.

  • No account required: You do not need to create an account to use DreamTone.
  • Local storage only: Your favorites, settings, and preferences are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. This data never leaves your device.
  • No analytics: We do not use analytics services to track your usage.

Third-Party Services

DreamTone uses the following third-party services:

  • Google AdMob: The free version displays banner advertisements provided by Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers and usage data as described in Google's Privacy Policy. We request non-personalized ads only.
  • RevenueCat: We use RevenueCat to manage in-app purchases and subscriptions. RevenueCat processes purchase transactions as described in RevenueCat's Privacy Policy.

In-App Purchases

DreamTone offers an optional premium subscription. Purchase transactions are handled by Apple (App Store) or Google (Google Play). We do not have access to your payment information.

Children's Privacy

DreamTone is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Data Retention

Since all app data is stored locally on your device, you can delete it at any time by clearing the app's data or uninstalling the app.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in the "Last updated" date above.
