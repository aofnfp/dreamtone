DreamTone is a beautiful sleep sounds app with a curated collection of ambient audio to help you relax and sleep better. Mix multiple sounds, set sleep timers, and drift off effortlessly.

Features:
• 20+ high-quality ambient sounds (rain, ocean, forest, white noise, and more)
• Mix and layer multiple sounds simultaneously
• Customizable sleep timer
• Volume control per sound
• Premium content available
• Offline playback — no internet required

Built by the Abraham Oladotun Foundation NFP.
